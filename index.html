Water Resources Department

The Water Conservancy Administration of the Ministry of Economic Affairs is an administrative agency under the Republic of China, in charge of the promotion of national rivers, water conservancy, and water resources-related businesses, as well as the formulation and implementation of policies and regulations.

  • One Director
  • 2 to 3 deputy directors
  • One person from the Chief Engineering Department
  • One chief secretary
